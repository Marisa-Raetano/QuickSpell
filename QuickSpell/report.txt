Reported Words With Definitions
motion
A state of progression from one place to another.
chair
An item of furniture used to sit on or in, comprising a seat, legs, back, and sometimes arm rests, for use by one person. Compare stool, couch, sofa, settee, loveseat and bench.
slave
A person who is held in servitude as the property of another person, and whose labor (and often also whose body and life) is subject to the owners volition and control.
wild
The undomesticated state of a wild animal
stop
A (usually marked) place where buses, trams or trains halt to let passengers get on and off, usually smaller than a station.
the
With a comparative or with more and a verb phrase, establishes a correlation with one or more other such comparatives.
order
Arrangement, disposition, or sequence.
